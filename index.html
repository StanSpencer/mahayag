About Mahayag

Mahayag is a municipality in the province of Zamboanga del Sur, located in the Zamboanga Peninsula region of the Philippines. Known for its peaceful rural environment, agricultural economy, and strong community culture, Mahayag represents the charm and opportunity of Mindanao’s developing municipalities.
